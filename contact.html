FAQ

Aksar Pooche Jaane Wale Sawaal

Admission ke liye kya karna hota hai?

Hamari website pe Admission Form bharo ya seedha coaching aao। Form mein student ka naam, pita ka naam, contact number, class aur gaanv ka naam chahiye। Aditya Sir 24 ghante mein contact karenge।

Fees kitni hai?

Fees class aur batch ke hisaab se alag hoti hai। Sahi fees jaanne ke liye Aditya Sir se WhatsApp ya call pe seedha poochhen। Hum affordable education mein believe karte hain।

YouTube classes kab upload hoti hain?

Har class ke baad lectures YouTube pe upload kiye jaate hain। Channel subscribe karein aur bell icon dabaayein taaki notification mile। Sabhi videos bilkul free hain।

Doubt session kab hota hai?

Har Ravivar subah 10 baje special free doubt clearing session hota hai। Iske alawa website ke Doubts section mein bhi apna sawaal submit kar sakte ho।

Kya online classes bhi milti hain?

Haan! Offline classes ke saath saath YouTube pe free online classes bhi available hain। Jo students door rehte hain woh YouTube se complete syllabus cover kar sakte hain।

Notes kaise milenge?

Enrolled students ko notes directly class mein milte hain। Website pe bhi jald hi notes upload honge। Tab tak WhatsApp pe contact karein।
